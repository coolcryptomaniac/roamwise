RoamWise Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 2026 · Last updated: 15 July 2026 · GYANVERSE, Almora, India

RoamWise ("we", "our") is built by Mohit Pandey in Almora, India. This policy explains what we collect, how ads and cookies work, and the choices you have — including specific rights for visitors in the EEA, UK and Switzerland.

What we collect

RoamWise runs primarily on your device. Searches, XP, saved lists, profile fields and journey logs are stored in your browser's local storage on your device only. If you create an account, we store via Google Firebase: your email address, display name, optional profile details you choose to enter (such as a WhatsApp number or travel preferences), and your Pro membership status. We do not sell your personal data. Weather uses approximate IP-based location, fetched transiently to answer your request and never stored.

Advertising & cookies

Our website displays ads served by Google AdSense. To do this, Google and its partners:

  • Use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads and measure performance.
  • May use your approximate location and browsing on this site to show more relevant ads, where you have consented.
  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalised advertising at aboutads.info/choices. Learn how Google uses data at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Your choices in the EEA, UK & Switzerland

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, we ask for your consent before using advertising cookies or personalised ads, via a Google-certified consent banner shown on your first visit. You can choose to consent, not consent, or manage your options in detail, and you can change your choice at any time by clearing your cookies or using the privacy options link when available. Under the GDPR/UK GDPR you also have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing — contact us using the details below to exercise these rights.

Payments

Payments are processed by UPI apps, Gumroad or other listed providers; we never receive or store your card, UPI or bank credentials — only a payment or license reference used to activate Pro.

Third-party services

We rely on: Google AdSense (advertising); Google Firebase (authentication and database); Wikipedia and Openverse (destination information and photos); Open-Meteo (weather and geocoding); OpenStreetMap and CARTO (map imagery); YouTube (embedded videos). Each processes only the data needed to fulfil your request, under its own privacy policy.

Children

RoamWise is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.

Account deletion

Delete your account anytime in-app: More → account → Delete my account, or see our account deletion page. Deletion removes your profile and cloud data permanently.

Changes

We may update this policy as our services evolve; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
