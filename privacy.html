RoamWise Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective: July 2026 · GYANVERSE, Almora, India

What we collect

RoamWise runs primarily on your device. Searches, XP, packing lists and journey logs are stored in local storage on your device only. If you create an account, we store via Google Firebase: your email address, display name, and your Pro membership status. We do not sell data, show third-party trackers, or collect location in the background (weather uses approximate IP location, fetched transiently and never stored).

Payments

Payments are processed by UPI apps or Gumroad; we never receive or store card, UPI or bank credentials — only a payment/license reference used to activate Pro.

Third-party services

Wikipedia (destination info/photos), Open-Meteo (weather, geocoding), OpenStreetMap (map imagery), Google Firebase (authentication, database). Each processes only the request data needed to respond.

Account deletion

Delete your account anytime in-app: More → account → Delete my account. Or see our account deletion page. Deletion removes your profile and cloud data permanently.
