Best Time to Visit Jodhpur: the Month-by-Month Truth

Jodhpur is a monsoon-rhythm destination — the year splits into a lush green season and a dry golden one. The numbers below are real — monthly weather computed from the ERA5 climate archive for Jodhpur's exact coordinates — so this page answers the question most guides dodge: which months are actually worth your leave days?

The short answer: aim for January, March, November, skip May if you can, and read the packing notes before you book. Post-monsoon months are the secret: everything is green, waterfalls are full, and crowds haven't returned.

❓ FAQ

What is the best time to visit Jodhpur?

January, March, November score highest on our weather-comfort index — daytime around 23.7°C with manageable rainfall. Post-monsoon months are the secret: everything is green, waterfalls are full, and crowds haven't returned.

Which month should I avoid in Jodhpur?

May is the toughest window (43°C days, ~1mm rain). Peak monsoon weeks can disrupt transport and close trails — build slack into plans.

What should I pack for Jodhpur?

Pack a compact umbrella or poncho, quick-dry layers, and waterproof covers for electronics.
