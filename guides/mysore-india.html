Best Time to Visit Mysore: the Month-by-Month Truth

Mysore is a warm-all-year destination where rainfall, not temperature, decides your best window. The numbers below are real — monthly weather computed from the ERA5 climate archive for Mysore's exact coordinates — so this page answers the question most guides dodge: which months are actually worth your leave days?

The short answer: aim for January, February, November, skip October if you can, and read the packing notes before you book. The dry season's first and last weeks offer peak weather at below-peak prices.

🎒 What to pack for Mysore

Pack light cottons, reef-safe sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and one light rain layer. Comfortable walking shoes beat everything else in your bag.

🗓️ How to plan the days

Give Mysore 3–5 focused days. Mornings for the headline sights (crowds arrive after 10), afternoons for neighbourhoods, evenings for food. ❓ FAQ

What is the best time to visit Mysore?

January, February, November score highest on our weather-comfort index — daytime around 28.1°C with manageable rainfall. The dry season's first and last weeks offer peak weather at below-peak prices.

Which month should I avoid in Mysore?

October is the toughest window (27.2°C days, ~240mm rain). Humidity peaks with the rains — book air-conditioned stays in the wet months.

What should I pack for Mysore?

Pack light cottons, reef-safe sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and one light rain layer.
