Best Time to Visit Shillong: the Month-by-Month Truth

Shillong is a monsoon-rhythm destination — the year splits into a lush green season and a dry golden one. The numbers below are real — monthly weather computed from the ERA5 climate archive for Shillong's exact coordinates — so this page answers the question most guides dodge: which months are actually worth your leave days?

The short answer: aim for January, November, December, skip June if you can, and read the packing notes before you book. Post-monsoon months are the secret: everything is green, waterfalls are full, and crowds haven't returned.

❓ FAQ

What is the best time to visit Shillong?

January, November, December score highest on our weather-comfort index — daytime around 15.2°C with manageable rainfall. Post-monsoon months are the secret: everything is green, waterfalls are full, and crowds haven't returned.

Which month should I avoid in Shillong?

June is the toughest window (24.6°C days, ~324mm rain). Peak monsoon weeks can disrupt transport and close trails — build slack into plans.

What should I pack for Shillong?

Pack a compact umbrella or poncho, quick-dry layers, and waterproof covers for electronics.
