🎒 What to pack for Munsiyari

Pack proper layers — a warm jacket even in shoulder months, sunscreen for altitude, and broken-in shoes. Altitude amplifies sun and cold at once — respect both.

🗓️ How to plan the days

Give Munsiyari 3–5 focused days. Mornings for the headline sights (crowds arrive after 10), afternoons for neighbourhoods, evenings for food. ❓ FAQ

What is the best time to visit Munsiyari?

June, July, August score highest on our weather-comfort index — daytime around 4.2°C with manageable rainfall. Shoulder months give you snow-capped views with open roads — the photographer's window.

Which month should I avoid in Munsiyari?

January is the toughest window (-12.4°C days, ~5mm rain). Winter access can close passes and roads; always check conditions before booking.

What should I pack for Munsiyari?

Pack proper layers — a warm jacket even in shoulder months, sunscreen for altitude, and broken-in shoes.
