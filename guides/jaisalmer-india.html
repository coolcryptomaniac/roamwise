Best Time to Visit Jaisalmer: the Month-by-Month Truth

Jaisalmer is a warm-all-year destination where rainfall, not temperature, decides your best window. The short answer: aim for January, March, November, skip May if you can, and read the packing notes before you book. The dry season's first and last weeks offer peak weather at below-peak prices.

🎒 What to pack for Jaisalmer

Pack light cottons, reef-safe sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and one light rain layer. Comfortable walking shoes beat everything else in your bag.

🗓️ How to plan the days

Give Jaisalmer 3–5 focused days. Mornings for the headline sights (crowds arrive after 10), afternoons for neighbourhoods, evenings for food. ❓ FAQ

What is the best time to visit Jaisalmer?

January, March, November score highest on our weather-comfort index — daytime around 21.8°C with manageable rainfall. The dry season's first and last weeks offer peak weather at below-peak prices.

Which month should I avoid in Jaisalmer?

May is the toughest window (42.8°C days, ~0mm rain). Humidity peaks with the rains — book air-conditioned stays in the wet months.

What should I pack for Jaisalmer?

Pack light cottons, reef-safe sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and one light rain layer.
